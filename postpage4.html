HikeHER Dallas Web Design

Date: September 2024

Background

Context: My third internship began in September of 2024 with a Dallas based social impact organization named HikeHER Dallas. I once again had the opportunity to work closely with the executive team for this organization and led the design team. HikeHer Dallas is all about empowering women through group hikes and creating meaningful outdoor experiences. They host fundraisers to uplift and support the breast cancer community with resources, care packages, and events. For their website redesign, I worked on creating pages for their blog, events, donations, sponsor sign-up, homepage, and a section about the founder, making sure their mission and values came through in every part of the site.

Role: UI/UX Design Intern

Brand: HikeHER Dallas

Project Type: Desktop Web Design with Figma and Development

UX/UI Design

After meeting with the founder of the organization and reviewing their current website, I began a low fidelity design of the company's homepage. They had an established following and wanted to provide more features that would make their website a one-stop platform for all things HikeHer Dallas. This is why I, along with the executive team, planned to have and create pages that provided all the information an interested or established user would want to browse; such as events, donation information, and a blog.

For the blog and events pages, I wanted to make upcoming, current, and past posts to be available to viewers. As well as, make it easy for them to access and add to their calendar, in the case of the events page. Below are the low and mid fidelity designs for the blog, events, and donations pages.


For the donations page, it was essential that it be impactful and clearly describe the company's mission and goals. I wanted to make it clear how funds would help and the resources they would be going to.
