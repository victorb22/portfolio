Online Learning Community Web Design with Figma

Date: March 2025

Background

Context: This web design project was developed for a business class, focusing on creating a social learning platform tailored for UC Davis students. Inspired by platforms like Skillshare, Coursera, and MasterClass, it was designed to support current UCD courses across various majors, offering a more specialized and campus-specific learning experience for students looking to explore different major options.

Role: UI/UX Designer

Project Type: UI/UX Design with Figma

UX Research

I started by creating a user persona based on a UC Davis student exploring different majors. To inform this, I interviewed four UCD students and found through research that over 50% of UC Davis undergraduates change their major. Using these insights, I developed a user persona that reflects the needs and challenges of students navigating their academic paths.

Low/Mid-Fidelity Designs

I then moved onto Figma, and began brainstorming what landing pages could look like for signed in users.
